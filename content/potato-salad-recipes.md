# Potato Salad Recipes: A Classic for Every Occasion

Cold, starchy, and (ideally) not swimming in regret. Here are potato salads good enough to forgive the existence of raisins in other, lesser salads. Whether it's for a summer picnic, a cozy family gathering, or a casual barbecue, potato salads are the unsung heroes of the table. So grab your potatoes, and let’s dig in.

## Classic Potato Salad Recipe

For those who cherish tradition, a **classic potato salad** wraps comfort in every bite. 

### Ingredients:
- 2 pounds of potatoes, preferably yellow or red
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
- 3 green onions, chopped
- 3 hard-boiled eggs, diced
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Paprika for garnish

### Instructions:
1. **Boil the Potatoes:** Cube the potatoes and place them in a pot of salted water. Boil until fork-tender, about 15 minutes. 
2. **Make the Dressing:** In a bowl, mix together mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, salt, and pepper.
3. **Combine:** Drain the potatoes and let them cool slightly. In a large bowl, mix the potatoes with the dressing, green onions, and hard-boiled eggs. 
4. **Chill:** Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to let the flavors meld.
5. **Serve:** Garnish with a sprinkle of paprika.

This **potato salad recipe** is refreshing, creamy, and holds up beautifully, making it a staple at any gathering.

## Healthy Potato Salad Variations

If you're wandering the path of health-conscious decisions and want something lighter, a **healthy potato salad** swaps heavy mayo for yogurt or vinaigrette. 

### Ingredients:
- 2 pounds of baby potatoes, halved
- 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- Fresh herbs like parsley or dill, chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Instructions:
1. **Cook the Potatoes:** Steam or boil the baby potatoes until tender.
2. **Prepare the Dressing:** In a bowl, whisk together Greek yogurt, olive oil, red wine vinegar, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
3. **Mix Together:** In a large bowl, combine the warm potatoes with the dressing and fold in the cherry tomatoes and fresh herbs.
4. **Chill:** Allow to cool in the fridge before serving.

This **healthy potato salad** keeps the spirit light while delivering a punch of flavor and freshness. 

## Unique Potato Salad Ideas

Tired of the same old? Let's shake things up with some unique spins on potato salad. Think beyond the usual suspects and get creative. 

### Ingredients:
- 2 pounds of purple potatoes
- 1/4 cup tahini
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1/4 cup olives, chopped
- 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
- Spinach or arugula for a fresh bite

### Instructions:
1. **Cook the Potatoes:** Boil or roast the purple potatoes until tender. Their vibrant color adds a stunning visual to any dish.
2. **Prepare the Dressing:** In a bowl, mix tahini, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
3. **Combine:** In a large bowl, mix the potatoes with the dressing, olives, and feta. Serve over a bed of spinach or arugula.

This intriguing potato salad variant brings a Mediterranean flair to your plate, leaving everyone asking for more.
